Notes

  • Search bar: I have incorporated a search bar in my website as it is a very important part of a well designed website. According to Jakob Nielsen more than 50% of all users go straight for the search bar when they enter a wesbite and do not want to look around. Due this it is extremely important to include a search bar. My search bar is not functioning as there is nothing to search in it, but I have included it to highlight how important a search bar is in website design.
  • Responsive: I have made sure my website is fully responsive/mobile friendly. This is hugely important due to the fact that a lot of people now use their mobile to browse the internet and would leave your website if it was not responsive and looked poorly designed on a mobile
  • Color scheme: I have used a color scheme of blue and white throughout my website, using consistent coloring for headings etc. This makes the design of the website look cohesive and more professional. The colors I made sure to choose colours which are not too saturated as saturated colours can be harsh on the users eyes.
  • Contact: Although it is not functioning I placed a contact form in my website as it is an important part of a well-designed website. Having a contact form is important as it important to make it easy for your users to contact you
  • Gestalt design: I have tried to follow Gestalt's Psychology for design by grouping items that are similar, grouping items that are close to each other, having good alignment and symmetry where possible (as this is important for order) and having whitespace in between sections to interupt the continuity.
